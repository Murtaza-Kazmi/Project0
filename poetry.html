Twinkling eyes,

Glimmering stars,

Ever so gentle zephyr,

And the serene sky,

All these,

Coupled with rapture,

Made it feel nothing less than a mystical experience..

Life had never felt this complete...

But then.....the stars started to fade away,

Darkness started to disappear,

The firmament's glint was overwhelmed,

As the sun finally kissed the horizon,

And for the first time in life,

It hadn't dawned on the earth,

As it dawned upon you,

.

.

LIFE HAD PASSED BY......
